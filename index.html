The Story

Built by one person
For real people

OYT started with a simple, uncomfortable question: where does my time actually go?

I'd end a week feeling busy but having nothing to show for it. I couldn't tell how many hours I'd spent at work versus home, or whether I'd made it to the gym as often as I thought. I wanted data. Not estimates, not calendar blocks. A real record of my life.

So I built OYT. What started as a personal experiment became a fully automatic time tracker that runs silently, turns your real-world movement into a timeline, and gives you the kind of honest life audit that actually changes behavior.

Jacob Wilson

Developer, OYT
