The Story

Built by one person.
For real people.

OYT started with a simple, uncomfortable question: where does my time actually go?

I'd end a week feeling busy but having nothing to show for it. I couldn't tell how many hours I'd spent at work versus home, or whether I'd actually made it to the gym as often as I thought. I wanted data — not estimates, not calendar blocks, but a real record of my life.

So I built OYT. What started as a personal experiment became a fully automatic time tracker that runs silently in the background, turns your real-world movement into a timeline, and gives you the kind of honest life audit that actually changes behavior.
