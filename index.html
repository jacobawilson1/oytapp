Features

Built around your real life

No manual check-ins. No forgotten logs. OYT runs quietly in the background and builds your day automatically.

📍
Automatic Location Tracking
Define your zones once — gym, office, home — and OYT logs them every visit without you lifting a finger.
🎯
Daily Goal Rings
Set targets for sleep, work, fitness, and more. Watch your personal rings fill as you move through the day.
📊
Weekly Life Audit
Every week, get a full breakdown of how your time actually split across every category in your life.
🚶
Smart Movement Detection
OYT knows the difference between driving, walking, and sitting still — and logs each one as its own category.
🗺️
Custom Zones
Save any place on the map as a named zone. OYT will automatically detect every visit — even past ones.
📔
Daily Journal
Attach quick thoughts, voice notes, or photos to any moment in your day — right inside your timeline.
