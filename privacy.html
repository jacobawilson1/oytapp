Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version

OYT stores all your data on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. Your location history, time blocks, and journal entries never leave your iPhone.

1. Who We Are

OYT ("Own Your Time") is an iOS application developed and maintained by Jake Wilson. If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, contact us at support@oytapp.com.

2. Data We Access

OYT requests access to the following data types on your device. Each permission is used exclusively to power the feature described — nothing else.

📍
Precise Location (Always On)

Detects when you arrive at or leave your saved zones and logs time automatically in the background. Required for the core tracking feature to work without you opening the app.

🚶
Motion & Fitness

Distinguishes between driving, walking, and stationary states so your activity timeline is accurate. Uses Apple's CoreMotion framework — no raw sensor data leaves the device.

❤️
Health Data (Read-Only)

Reads step count, sleep analysis, and workout data from Apple Health to enrich and categorize your time blocks. OYT never writes to or modifies your Health data.

📅
Calendar (Read-Only)

Displays your planned calendar events alongside your tracked time. OYT never creates, edits, or deletes calendar events.

📷
Camera & Microphone

Used only inside the Journal feature — to scan documents, capture photos, or record voice notes attached to your time blocks. Media is stored locally and never uploaded.

🖼️
Photos

Used only inside the Journal feature to attach images from your photo library to journal entries. Images are stored on your device only.

🎙️
Speech Recognition

Used inside the Journal feature to transcribe voice notes. Transcription is handled by Apple's on-device Speech framework and is not sent to third-party services.

🔔
Notifications

Used to surface optional reminders and goal nudges — such as alerting you when you're close to hitting a weekly target. All notifications are generated locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed by OYT is used exclusively to power features within the app itself:

  • Location data is used to detect zone arrivals/departures and create time blocks in your timeline.
  • Motion data is used to improve tracking accuracy — distinguishing walking from driving, for example.
  • Health data is used to enrich time blocks with context such as sleep duration and workout activity.
  • Camera, microphone, photo, and speech data is used only when you explicitly activate Journal features.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose outside the app's core functionality.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled for OYT, your data may sync via iCloud to your other Apple devices — this is governed by Apple's iCloud privacy policy and is encrypted end-to-end.

OYT does not operate any servers or databases that receive your personal data. Your location history, time blocks, zones, and journal entries never leave your device outside of iCloud sync.

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security — Face ID, Touch ID, passcode encryption, and the Secure Enclave.

5. Third-Party Sharing

OYT does not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or crash reporting services embedded in the app that collect personal data.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data lives on your device for as long as OYT is installed. To delete all data permanently, delete the OYT app from your iPhone. This removes all stored time blocks, zones, journal entries, and preferences.

If iCloud sync is enabled, deleting the app and its associated iCloud data from Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage will remove all synced data.

7. Children's Privacy

OYT is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used OYT and you have concerns, contact us at support@oytapp.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and notify users via an in-app notice on the next app launch after the change takes effect.
