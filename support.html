Common Questions

📍
Why does OYT need "Always On" location access?

OYT detects when you arrive at or leave your saved zones (home, work, gym, etc.) and automatically logs your time in the background. Without "Always On" access, tracking only works while the app is open. Your location data never leaves your device.

🔋
Will OYT drain my battery?

OYT uses Apple's geofencing APIs, which are designed to be extremely battery-efficient. The system only wakes OYT when you cross a zone boundary. It does not continuously poll your GPS. Most users see no measurable impact on battery life.

😴
My sleep isn't showing up. What do I do?

OYT reads sleep data from Apple Health. Make sure your Apple Watch, Oura, or Whoop is syncing sleep to the Health app, then open OYT and pull down to refresh. You can also log sleep manually by tapping the sleep card on the Today tab.

🏠
How do I set up my home and work zones?

Go to the Zones tab, tap the + button, and drop a pin at your location. Give it a name and assign it a category (Home, Work, Gym, etc.). OYT will automatically start logging time there whenever your device enters that area.

📊
What is the OYT Score?

Your OYT Score (0–100) measures how well you owned your day. It's calculated from your core goals (sleep, work), flexible goals (gym, hobbies), and time diversity, rewarding a balanced, intentional day even without formal goals set for every category.

🔒
Is my data private? Does OYT send anything to a server?

No. All your data (location history, time blocks, journal entries, and goals) is stored entirely on your device. OYT has no servers and does not transmit your personal data anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

🗑️
How do I delete my data?

Delete the OYT app from your iPhone. This permanently removes all stored data. If you had iCloud enabled, also remove OYT data from Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

💳
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → OYT. You can cancel, change your plan, or view your billing date there. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
